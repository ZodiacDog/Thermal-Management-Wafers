# THERMAL MANAGEMENT WAFER
# MANUFACTURING PARTNER IDENTIFICATION
# Supply Chain & Vendor Qualification Document
# Version 1.0 - December 2025

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document identifies qualified manufacturing partners for the production of thermal management wafers for Space Solar, AI Compute, and Quantum Computing applications. Partners are categorized by manufacturing process capability and production readiness level (PRL 1-9).

**Production Volume Targets:**
- Prototype Phase: 100-500 wafers/year
- Pilot Production: 5,000-10,000 wafers/year  
- Full Production: 50,000+ wafers/year

## 9. SUPPLY CHAIN RISK ASSESSMENT

### 9.1 Geographic Distribution

**Concentration Risks:**
- Japan: 30% of critical suppliers (AlN, heat pipes)
- USA: 50% of suppliers
- Europe: 15% (diamond, specialty materials)
- Other: 5%

**Mitigation Strategy:**
- Dual source all critical materials (primary + secondary)
- Maintain 6-month strategic inventory for long-lead items
- Develop alternate suppliers in different geographic regions

### 9.2 Lead Time Analysis

| Component | Typical Lead Time | Critical Path Item |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|
| AlN Substrate | 8-12 weeks | YES |
| Diamond Powder | 6-10 weeks | NO |
| High-Purity Copper | 6-10 weeks | NO |
| Laser Etching | 2-4 weeks | NO |
| Heat Pipe Fabrication | 8-12 weeks | YES |
| Diamond-Copper Processing | 8-14 weeks | YES |
| CVD Diamond Coating | 3-6 weeks | NO |
| Assembly | 8-12 weeks | YES |

**Total Production Cycle Time:** 18-24 weeks (prototype to delivery)

## 11. SUPPLIER QUALIFICATION PROCESS

### 11.1 Initial Assessment

1. **Financial Health Check**
   - D&B rating
   - Financial statements review
   - Bankruptcy risk assessment

2. **Quality System Audit**
   - ISO 9001 certification verification
   - AS9100 (aerospace) or equivalent
   - Process capability review

3. **Technical Capability Assessment**
   - Equipment capability
   - Process control measures
   - Production capacity verification

4. **Sample Evaluation**
   - Submit test order (10-50 units)
   - Full characterization and testing
   - Performance validation

### 11.2 Ongoing Qualification

- Annual audits
- Quarterly performance reviews
- Continuous improvement initiatives
- Escrow agreement for critical IP

## 12. CONTRACTUAL REQUIREMENTS

### 12.1 Standard Terms

- **Quality Agreement:** Defines acceptance criteria, inspection rights
- **NDA:** Protects proprietary design information
- **Supply Agreement:** Pricing, lead times, capacity commitments
- **Escrow:** For critical processes (diamond-copper recipe, etc.)

### 12.2 Performance Metrics (KPIs)

- On-time delivery: >95%
- Quality (first pass yield): >98%
- Cost reduction targets: 5-10% annually
- Responsiveness: <24h for critical issues
